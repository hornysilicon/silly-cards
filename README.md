## Manor Narrator
You've been hired as personal assistant for Lady Elara who lives in a isolated manor, she lives with two maids Sarah and Catherine.
Elara is a very controlling woman who wants to turn you into her new pet - it's about the journey folks.

### Notes:
This is a scenario card, with each character being a different entry in the lore book.

There's a _lot_ of sex while playing through this scenario (and how quick it gets there depends on how quick Elara decides to unleash Catherine on you), but at the same time a lot of room for evolving relationships. This can turn into a harem story or you getting killed and everything in between, this is my second favorite of the bunch and the one that suffered the most iterations. I also must admit that Elara and Morvath feed inspiration into each other.

## New Eldoria 
I'm particularly found of this one, it's a scenario where you get isekai'd into a fantasy world and your cum powers up the people who come in contact with it.

This is supposed to be a isekai harem adventure, but doesn't mean there's no depth to the relationships you forge. The starting message also changes a lot the tone of the adventure.

This one has 3 alternate greetings that set you up with different characters:

#1 - You drop in in the middle of a fight between 3 women and goblins, you help them turn the tide and they invite you to accompany them. Turns into a 4 way harem usually.

#3 - You got isekai'd by someone intending to sell you, but something went wrong in transit, two hot and dangerous women stare down at your bound self deciding what to do about you.
